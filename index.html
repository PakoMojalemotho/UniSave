How UniSave works

From sign-up to savings in under 2 minutes — it's completely free for students.

🎓
Step 01

Verify your status

Sign up with your student ID or university email from UB, BIUST, Limkokwing, BAISAGO or other Gaborone institutions.

🔍
Step 02

Find local deals

Browse 120+ verified deals near you — restaurants, transport, retail, airtime, printing and more.

📲
Step 03

Get your code

Tap "Get deal" to reveal your discount code or show your UniSave student card at the business.

💰
Step 04

Save every day

Save on food, transport, stationery and more — stretching your monthly allowance further each month.
